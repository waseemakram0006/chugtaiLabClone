Our Story

With over 4 decades of excellence, Chughtai Lab has been a pioneer in diagnostic services in Pakistan. Since 2021, we’ve been at the forefront of innovation in Healthcare expanding beyond Chughtai Institute of Pathology to offer a diverse range of services including Community Education, Diagnostics, Radiology, Vaccination Centers, Pharmacy, Clinics, Ambulance Services, Homecare, Medical College, and a Teaching Hospital. Since our establishment, we’ve consistently prioritized patients with prompt results and accurate diagnoses. We’ve accomplished a milestone of nationwide presence with over 350 collection centers across Pakistan. Our lab operates 24/7, providing accessible services for everyone.
